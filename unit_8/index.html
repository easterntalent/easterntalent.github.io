    Welcome to the
    Eastern Talent Agency

    We represent highly accomplished Below The Line artists for Film and Television. Agent Maureen Toth and her team, Scott Benson, Courtney Stein and Jen Caprio represent Editors, Directors of Photography, First Assistant Directors, Production Designers, Costume Designers, Hair and Make-Up.

    Our main office is located in the heart of downtown Los Angeles across from the renowned Orpheum Theater in the beautiful Eastern Columbia Building, and our New York office is located in the historic DUMBO neighborhood of Brooklyn.
